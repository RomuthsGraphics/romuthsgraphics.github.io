HOW TO ADD YOUR WORKS:
----------------------

1. RECENT WORKS (works/recent/)
   - Add your most recent projects here
   - Recommended: 3-6 images
   - Best size: 800x600 pixels or 4:3 ratio
   - Formats: .jpg, .png, .webp

2. SHOWCASE (works/showcase/)
   - Featured projects you want to highlight
   - Your best work goes here
   - Best size: 1200x800 pixels

3. BRANDING (works/branding/)
   - Logo designs
   - Brand identity projects
   - Business cards, letterheads

4. UI/UX (works/ui-ux/)
   - Web designs
   - App mockups
   - User interface designs

5. ILLUSTRATION (works/illustration/)
   - Digital art
   - Custom illustrations
   - Character designs

NAMING CONVENTION:
------------------
Use descriptive names like:
- project1-logo-design.jpg
- project2-website-mockup.png
- client-name-branding.jpg

TIPS:
-----
- Keep images optimized (under 500KB each)
- Use consistent image sizes for best display
- Add at least 3 images per category
